## 🇫🇮 Yritysvälitys Suomi Platform

This project was built for **Yritysvälitys Suomi**, a platform connecting business buyers and sellers in Finland.

The goal was to make company acquisitions more transparent and accessible by giving users a clear way to find, compare, and broker businesses.

---

## ⚙️ Project Focus

The delivered product includes:

- 🏠 A polished landing page
- 🛠️ A dedicated control panel experience
- 🔎 Clear presentation of business listing and brokering purpose

---

## 🎯 Final Result

The platform positions Yritysvälitys Suomi as a practical digital channel for business transfer activities in Finland, supporting both discoverability and management workflows in one solution. 🚀
